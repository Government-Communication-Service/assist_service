Educational Programs and Training

This program is designed for males and females aged 16-25 years who want to learn about technology. Applications should be submitted by midnight on the 31st December.

Participants will learn about artificial intelligence, machine learning, etc. The course covers various topics, eg. programming, data analysis, ie. the fundamental skills needed for a career in technology.

The program was launched by the Minister of Education at the Technology Academy in London. All participants receive a Certificate in Technology Skills upon completion.
