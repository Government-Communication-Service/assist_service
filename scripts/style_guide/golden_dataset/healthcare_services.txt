Healthcare and Wellbeing Services

The new healthcare initiative was launched by the government to improve access to medical services for all citizens regardless of their background, and the program has been designed to address longstanding inequalities in healthcare provision across different regions, and it will provide additional funding to hospitals and clinics in underserved areas, and patients will benefit from reduced waiting times and improved quality of care.

Appointments are available between 8am-6pm. Medical records can be accessed through the patient portal, where he can view his test results and prescription history.

The program will impact thousands of families and foster better health outcomes across the nation.
