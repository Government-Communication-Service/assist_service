Transport and Infrastructure

The new rail service operates from 0600hrs to 2200hrs daily. Services run at fifteen minute intervals during peak times (7am-9am and 5pm-7pm).

The project was funded by the Department for Transport and will streamline travel across the region. The key aim is to facilitate better connections and leverage existing infrastructure to deploy an integrated transport hub.

Passenger feedback has been overwhelmingly positive; the service has reduced journey times; regular commuters appreciate the improved reliability.

Tickets cost £5.50 for adults and £2.75 for children. Annual passes are available for £1,200.00.
