Social Care and Community Support

The program provides support for vulnerable individuals including elderly people, disabled people, and BAME communities. Services are available 24/7 with emergency response times averaging 30mins.

Caregivers receive comprehensive training through the National Care Academy. Each participant must complete Two hundred hours of supervised practice before qualification.

The Director of Social Services emphasized that the initiative will foster community cohesion; it will combat social isolation; participants will benefit from enhanced support networks.

If someone needs assistance, he or she should call the helpline at 0800-123-4567.
