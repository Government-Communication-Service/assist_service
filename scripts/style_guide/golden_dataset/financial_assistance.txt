Financial Assistance Programs

Individuals can apply for Universal Credit, Tax Credits, Pension Credit, etc. The application process has been simplified to make it easier for citizens to access the support they need.

Payments are processed on the 1st of each month at 00:00. Late applications will not be accepted after 12 noon on the deadline date.

Your organization must provide an official stamp where appropriate when filling out forms. Applicants must provide color photocopies of their documents and ensure all information is correctly labeled.

Benefits are calculated based on household income; couples receive higher payments than single individuals; additional support is available for families with children.
