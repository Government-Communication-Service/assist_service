Employment Rights and Workplace Policies

All employees are entitled to fair treatment regardless of their gender, ethnicity, or background. The policy applies to both males and females working in all departments.

Working hours are 9am-5pm with a one hour lunch break. Overtime is paid at 1.5x the standard rate. Employees can accrue up to thirty vacation days per year.

The Human Resources Director announced these changes at the Staff Meeting in Conference Room A. Questions should be directed to your Line Manager or the HR Department.

The company is committed to promoting diversity & inclusion; this includes supporting ethnic minorities and ensuring equal opportunities for all.
