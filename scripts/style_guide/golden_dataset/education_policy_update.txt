Education Policy Update 2025

The Department For Education has announced reforms to A-levels and A-Level qualifications. Students achieving a-star grades will receive recognition.

The Academies Programme continues to expand with new Academy Trust partnerships. The Business Plan includes support for academy converters across regions.

Disabled students can access support including Disability living allowance and special provisions. We must avoid referring to 'the disabled' in our materials.

New guidance on e-mail communication has been published. Staff should use email addresses ending in gov.uk not Gov.Uk.

The Director General met with the Chair of Governors to discuss funding. The director-general emphasized the importance of careworkers in schools.

The Cabinet approved new measures yesterday. This represents significant progress towards our goals; we aim to transform educational outcomes.

Schools offering A* qualifications must ensure proper support systems. Contact your Local Authority for more information about the programme.

International students from the US can apply through special pathways. The devolved Governments have agreed to coordinate policies.
