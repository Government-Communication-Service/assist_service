How Can We Modernize Government Services?

The GDS is working to utilize cutting-edge technologies to deliver seamless digital experiences. The project will tackle outdated systems and streamline processes to create a one-stop-shop portal for all government services.

The initiative aims to empower citizens by providing easy access to services, eg. passport renewals, driving licenses, tax returns, etc. Users can liaise with departments through the online platform.

Support is available 9am-5pm Monday-Friday. After-hours assistance can be accessed through the automated help-line.
