Business Support and Grants

Small businesses can access government funding through various channels, eg. innovation grants, startup loans, etc. The application process requires detailed financial information, ie. profit and loss statements, balance sheets, and cash flow projections.

The program is designed to incentivize entrepreneurship and facilitate business growth. Companies can leverage these funds to deploy new technologies and transform their operations going forward.

Funding decisions are typically made within 6-8 weeks. Successful applicants will be notified by B.B.C. (Business Banking Channel).

The deadline for Quarter one applications is the 31st March.
