Housing and Local Services

THE COUNCIL HAS ANNOUNCED NEW HOUSING INITIATIVES TO TACKLE THE SHORTAGE OF AFFORDABLE HOMES.

The program aims to deliver over 5000 new properties by 2027; these will include social housing, affordable rentals, and shared ownership schemes; the initiative represents a robust commitment to addressing housing needs; it will transform communities and empower local residents.

The Minister for Housing made the announcement at the Housing Authority headquarters. Each property will be built to high environmental standards & will include modern amenities.

Interested applicants should contact the Housing Department between 9-5pm, Mon-Fri.
