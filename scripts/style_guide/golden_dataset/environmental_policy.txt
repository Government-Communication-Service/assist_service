Environmental Policy and Sustainability

The Government's environmental strategy aims to combat climate change through a comprehensive program of initiatives that will reduce carbon emissions across all sectors of the economy, and the plan includes investments in renewable energy, improvements to public transport, and incentives for businesses to adopt greener practices, and these measures are expected to have a significant impact on the UK's ability to meet its international commitments under the Paris Agreement.

The Department for Environment is leading this work in collaboration with the Ministry of Energy. The Secretary of State announced the strategy at Parliament yesterday.

Implementation will begin at .5 degrees warming threshold. The budget allocation is £2,500,000,000 over 5 years.
