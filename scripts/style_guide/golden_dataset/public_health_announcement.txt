PUBLIC HEALTH ANNOUNCEMENT

THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH HAS ISSUED NEW GUIDELINES FOR PREVENTING THE SPREAD OF INFECTIOUS DISEASES.

All Citizens Should Follow These Recommendations:
- Wash hands regularly
- Maintain social distancing
- Wear masks in crowded spaces; etc.

The guidelines apply to everyone including children, adults, the elderly, BAME populations, etc. Information is available in multiple languages on the Government Website.

The announcement was made by the Chief Medical Officer at 12 noon today. Further updates will be provided at 6:00pm tonight.

For more information, visit the online Hub or contact your local Health Authority.
