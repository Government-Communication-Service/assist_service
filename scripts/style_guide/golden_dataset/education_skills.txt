Education and Skills Development

The Academy offers courses in various subjects including Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. Classes are held in the Main Building on Campus.

Students aged eighteen to twenty-five can enroll in the program, which includes modules on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Analytics. The curriculum was designed by the Head of Department to meet industry standards.

Graduates receive a Diploma in Advanced Technology Skills. If a student has questions, he should speak to his academic advisor. Males and females are equally encouraged to apply.

Application deadline is 11:59pm on the 30th September.
