Technology and Innovation

The R&D (Research & Development) department has developed innovative solutions using A.I. and M.L. technologies. The NAO reviewed the project and found it to be highly effective.

The system can analyze large datasets, optimize workflows, and provide real-time insights, ie. it offers comprehensive business intelligence capabilities that will transform decision-making processes going forward.

Development costs totaled £1500000 over three years. The project runs on servers consuming 500KB-900KB of memory per transaction.

The agile development methodology was used throughout the project lifecycle; teams collaborated across multiple locations; the results demonstrate the value of this approach.
