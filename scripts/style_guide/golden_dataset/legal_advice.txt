Can You Get Free Legal Advice?

Citizens may be eligible for legal aid depending on their financial circumstances, and the assessment process takes into account income, savings, and other assets, and applicants must provide comprehensive documentation including bank statements, payslips, and proof of expenses, and the evaluation can take several weeks to complete depending on the complexity of the case.

Legal aid covers various types of cases, eg. family law, housing disputes, immigration matters, etc. The program was reformed in 2012; it now operates under stricter eligibility criteria; many people who previously qualified no longer receive support.

Applications must be submitted by 00:00 on the deadline date. Processing times average 6-8wks.

For more information, contact the Legal Aid Agency or visit their online Portal.
